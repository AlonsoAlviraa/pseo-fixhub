Error Code E15

Fixing Bosch Error E15

Official troubleshooting guide for Bosch Dishwasher (Serie 6 Series)

Diagnostic Summary

Severity High
Est. Cost $50 - $150

How to Fix It

  1. 1

    Turn off the dishwasher immediately.

  2. 2

    Check for water in the base pan.

  3. 3

    Remove excess water with a sponge.

  4. 4

    Tilt the dishwasher slightly to drain water.

  5. 5

    Check for leaks in the door seal or hoses.
