Error Code E1/F9

Fixing Whirlpool Error E1/F9

Official troubleshooting guide for Whirlpool Dishwasher (Gold Series Series)

Diagnostic Summary

Severity High
Est. Cost $60 - $140
Safety First: Disconnect power and water supplies before opening panels. Keep floors dry to prevent slips.

What This Error Looks Like

Likely Symptoms

  • Intermittent performance or repeated code E1/F9 after a reset
  • Cycles stopping unexpectedly before completion
  • Unusual noises or vibrations during operation
  • Water remaining in the tub or slow draining after a cycle
  • Persistent odor from standing water between washes

Common Root Causes

  • Temporary glitch cleared by a full power cycle
  • Blocked air or water pathways reducing flow efficiency
  • Loose connectors or aging components that need reseating
  • Drain pump struggling because of clogs or kinked hoses

Tools & Prep Checklist

  • Phillips and flathead screwdrivers
  • Microfiber towels for cleanup
  • Smartphone flashlight or headlamp
  • Bucket or shallow pan for capturing residual water
  • Needle-nose pliers for clamps and hoses
  • Replacement parts specific to your dishwasher model

Step 3

Keep the Error From Coming Back

  • Log the date of this repair so you can identify recurring patterns over time.
  • Run a quick rinse or empty cycle monthly to keep sensors clean.
  • Inspect power cords and hoses for visible wear whenever you move the appliance.
Need help? If the error returns after completing these steps twice, contact a certified technician to check the control board, pump, and wiring harness.
